Dr. Norman Borlaug

A hero to the world

Here is some info on him

  • Fact #1 - He led initiatives worldwide that contributed to the extensive increases in agricultural production termed the Green Revolution.
  • Fact #2 - He received his B.S. in forestry in 1937 and Ph.D. in plant pathology and genetics from the University of Minnesota in 1942.
  • Fact #3 - He was often called "the father of the Green Revolution", and is credited with saving over a billion people worldwide from starvation.
